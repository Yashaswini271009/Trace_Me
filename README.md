TraceMe 🔍
# Digital Footprint Visualizer | Cyberstalking Awareness Project
TraceMe is an interactive web application that demonstrates how cyberstalkers can build a surprisingly detailed profile of a person using only publicly available information.

The project was developed as part of a Digital Forensics and Cybersecurity awareness initiative to help users understand the risks of excessive online exposure and learn practical ways to protect themselves.

## Why TraceMe?
Most people believe they are safe online because they don't share sensitive information directly.
In reality, attackers often rely on *Open Source Intelligence (OSINT)* techniques to gather small pieces of publicly available information and combine them into a complete profile of a target.
TraceMe makes this invisible process visible.

## Features
-🌐 Digital Exposure Map
Interactive graph visualization showing how information is exposed across different online platforms.
-📅 Stalker's Timeline
A realistic 7-day simulation demonstrating how an attacker can gradually build a complete profile using publicly available data.
-📊 Risk Score Dashboard
Measures exposure across multiple categories:
---Location Exposure
---Identity Exposure
---Routine Exposure
---Social Network Exposure
-✅ Protection Checklist
Provides actionable recommendations to reduce digital exposure and improve online privacy.
-📱 Shareable Awareness Card
Generate a personalized summary card that can be shared on LinkedIn and other platforms to spread cybersecurity awareness.

## How It Works
1. User enters a basic persona.
2. TraceMe simulates publicly available online information.
3. Data points are linked across multiple platforms.
4. Exposure risks are visualized through interactive dashboards.
5. A risk score is generated.
6. Protection recommendations are provided.
*Important*: No real user data is collected, stored, or processed.
